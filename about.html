About Us

BT House of Carpentry & Shopfitting delivers premium craftsmanship for residential and commercial interiors. Based in Free State, Bloemfontein, we specialize in bespoke finishes, high-quality fittings, and functional spaces designed for luxury living and modern business environments.

Our services include Tilling, Paving, Plastering, Roofing and additional finishing trades aligned to interior and structural enhancement. We take pride in detail, precision, and long-lasting workmanship.

From homes to retail spaces, we build environments that reflect quality, beauty, and functionality.
